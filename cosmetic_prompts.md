# Cosmetic Asset Prompts

Use these prompts in an AI image generator (like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion) to create high-quality assets for your app. 

**Tips for best results:**
*   **Aspect Ratio:** Use 1:1 (Square).
*   **Background:** Ask for a "solid white background" or "solid black background" to easily remove it later, or "transparent background" if the tool supports it.
*   **Style:** "3D glossy icon, vibrant colors, cute cartoon style, high quality render".
